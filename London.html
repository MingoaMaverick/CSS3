London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

London contains heritage sites such as; Greenwich, Tower of Londer, Palace of Westminster, and St Margaret's, Westminster Abber. From around 1831 to 1925 London was the world's most populous city.
