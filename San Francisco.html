☝ San Francisco

San Francisco also known as "The City by the Bay" is the fourth most populous city in California.San Francisco is a popular tourist destination. It is known for it's famous landmarks such as Alcatraz, Golden Gate Bridge, Fishermans Wharf, and other places.

San Francisco was founded on 1776. Also Sas Francisco is known for having destructive earthquakes such as the earthquake that happened in 1906.
